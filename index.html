🔥 The Official Roast 🔥

Randy's turning 49... which means he's been losing at fantasy football for at least 20 of those years. At this point it's not a hobby, it's a personality flaw.
They call him "El Jefe" because he learned how to say "Dos cervezas, por favor" on a Cancun trip in 2009 and has never let anyone forget it.
Father of 5 kids. That man looked at the chaos of 4 children and said "you know what this house needs? MORE." Respect? No. Concern? Absolutely.
Randy's idea of a gourmet meal is anything he can light on fire on a hibachi grill. The man treats soy sauce like it's a food group.
49 years old and still setting his fantasy lineup like a man who genuinely believes "this is the year." Spoiler alert: it is not the year.
Somewhere right now, Randy is standing in front of his grill in cargo shorts, white New Balances, and a phone holster... telling no one in particular that "it needs 2 more minutes."
